Some Hidden Fonts in Google Fonts


Google Fonts is a great resource, and its API for websites is used by this website. However, three traditional Chinese fonts are hidden. To be honest, I don't think most of my English-speaking audience would care, but since there is a Chinese version, might as well translate it.

According to Wayback Machine, in 2016, five Traditional Chinese fonts were tested in Early Access. They are in order: cwTeXFangSong（仿宋）, cwTeXHei（黑體）, cwTeXKai（楷體）, cwTeXMing（明體）, and cwTeXYen（圓體）. Among them, cwTeXHei（黑體）and cwTeXMing（明體）were superseded by Noto Sans Traditional Chinese and Noto Serif Traditional Chinese.


Here are samples of these fonts:

cwTeXKai:
南去經三國，東來過五湖
cwTeXYen:
南去經三國，東來過五湖
cwTeXFangSong:
南去經三國，東來過五湖
